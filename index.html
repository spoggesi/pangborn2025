About Me

I am a Research Officer in Sensory Analysis at the Feast Lab, Massey University (Ateaoroa-New Zealand). My research focuses on consumer perception, aroma chemistry, and sensory methodology development. I aim to bridge food science with consumer behaviour through robust and reproducible sensory methods.

Research Interests

  • Consumer sensory evaluation
  • Descriptive analysis & lexicon development
  • Aroma compounds and food volatiles
  • Cross-modal perception and product optimisation
